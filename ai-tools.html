Next-Generation AI Tools for Biocatalysis

We're building AI tools to plan, evaluate, and execute the use of biocatalysis in molecule synthesis. Our next-generation tools accelerate and de-risk biocatalysis, enabling cheaper, faster, and more sustainable manufacturing.

RetroBioCat Platform

Our AI platform for retrosynthetic analysis of biocatalytic routes.

The RetroBioCat platform is a powerful tool for designing biocatalytic routes to target molecules. It uses advanced algorithms to analyze retrosynthetic pathways, considering enzyme availability, reaction conditions, and process constraints to identify the most promising routes.
