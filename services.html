Our Services

Building on decades of biocatalysis know-how, we offer a range of lab services.

Comprehensive Biocatalysis Solutions

We offer a range of services to help you implement biocatalysis in your processes, from enzyme panel construction to reaction optimization.

Route Consultancy

We offer expert consultancy on biocatalytic retrosynthesis to help you identify where biocatalysis can have the greatest impact on your synthetic routes.

Our team of experienced scientists can analyze your synthetic routes or molecule to identify opportunities where biocatalytic steps can improve efficiency, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness. We provide tailored recommendations based on the latest developments in biocatalysis and our deep understanding of enzyme capabilities.

Enzyme Screening

Our high-throughput screening capabilities help you identify the best enzymes for your specific reactions.

With access to a whole host of analytical techniques, we can rapidly screen enzyme panels to find the most suitable candidates for your biocatalytic processes. We have access to plate-readers, UPLC-DAD, UPLC-ELSD, LC-MS, GC-FID & GC-MS, amongst other equipment. Get in touch to discuss assays. In addition, we have a close working relationship with Prozomix and can quickly screen their enzyme panels against your substrate. We also offer bespoke enzyme panel development, with a range of bioinformatics techniques available to select enzymes for testing, from maximising diversity with a given sequence space, through to more targeted structure guided approaches.

Enzyme Engineering

We engineer enzymes to improve their performance, stability, and specificity for your target reactions.

Our enzyme engineering capabilities combine computational design with directed evolution approaches to create enzymes with enhanced properties. We can optimize enzymes for improved activity, selectivity, stability, and tolerance to process conditions, enabling more efficient and robust biocatalytic processes.

Reaction Optimization

We optimize reaction conditions to maximize yield, selectivity, and efficiency for your biocatalytic processes.

Our reaction optimization services help you develop robust and scalable biocatalytic processes. We systematically evaluate and optimize key parameters such as pH, temperature, substrate concentration, co-factor regeneration, and reaction media to maximize performance and ensure successful implementation at scale. We employ iterative Design of Experiments (DoE) to explore multiple experimental factors simultaneously, allowing us to identify the optimal conditions for your specific reaction. Furthermore we have access to an EasyMax 102 allowing us to generate data under conditions relevant scale-up conditions.
