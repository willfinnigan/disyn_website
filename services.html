Our Services

Building on decades of biocatalysis know-how, we offer a range of lab services.

Comprehensive Biocatalysis Solutions

We offer a range of services to help you implement biocatalysis in your processes, from enzyme panel construction to reaction optimization.

Enzyme Panel Construction

We build custom enzyme panels tailored to your specific needs, leveraging our extensive library and expertise.

Our enzyme panels are designed to provide a diverse set of biocatalysts for your specific reaction types, allowing you to quickly identify the best enzymes for your processes. We carefully select enzymes based on their activity, selectivity, stability, and other key parameters to ensure optimal performance.

Screening

Our high-throughput screening capabilities help you identify the best enzymes for your specific reactions.

We employ state-of-the-art screening technologies to rapidly evaluate enzyme performance across a range of conditions. Our screening protocols are designed to identify enzymes with the desired activity, selectivity, and stability for your specific applications, saving you time and resources in the development process.

High-Throughput Methods

Rapid evaluation of multiple enzymes and conditions

Enzyme Engineering

We engineer enzymes to improve their performance, stability, and specificity for your target reactions.

Our enzyme engineering capabilities combine computational design with directed evolution approaches to create enzymes with enhanced properties. We can optimize enzymes for improved activity, selectivity, stability, and tolerance to process conditions, enabling more efficient and robust biocatalytic processes.

Computational Design

Directed Evolution

Reaction Optimization

We optimize reaction conditions to maximize yield, selectivity, and efficiency for your biocatalytic processes.

Our reaction optimization services help you develop robust and scalable biocatalytic processes. We systematically evaluate and optimize key parameters such as pH, temperature, substrate concentration, co-factor regeneration, and reaction media to maximize performance and ensure successful implementation at scale.
