About Disyn Biotec

We're on a mission to make biocatalysis simpler, faster, and easier to implement.

Our Mission

At Disyn Biotec, we are on a mission to make biocatalysis simpler, faster, and easier to implement. We believe that biocatalysis has the potential to revolutionize chemical manufacturing, making it more sustainable, efficient, and cost-effective.

Building on decades of biocatalysis know-how, we offer a range of lab services and are developing cutting-edge AI tools to accelerate and de-risk the implementation of biocatalysis in chemical synthesis.

Meet Our Team

Our team brings together expertise in biocatalysis, enzyme engineering, synthetic biology, and artificial intelligence.
